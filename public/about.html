About VoicePunjabAI

VoicePunjabAI is a Punjabi language tool built to make Punjabi text-to-speech simple, fast, and easy to use. It allows users to generate natural Punjabi voice audio from Gurmukhi text, translate English into Punjabi, and type Punjabi using an on-screen keyboard.

The platform is designed for creators, learners, families, educators, and anyone who wants to work with Punjabi audio more easily. Users can create MP3 files for personal use, practice, learning, voice content, and simple audio projects.

What VoicePunjabAI Offers

Punjabi Text-to-Speech

Convert Punjabi Gurmukhi text into natural-sounding MP3 audio.

English to Punjabi Translation

Translate English text into Punjabi before generating speech.

Punjabi Keyboard

Type Punjabi directly with the built-in keyboard and fix spelling quickly.

Fast Audio Output

Generate, play, and download Punjabi MP3 audio in a simple workflow.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make Punjabi voice technology more accessible online. VoicePunjabAI aims to support Punjabi content creation and make Punjabi text easier to listen to, share, and use in digital projects.
