Privacy Policy

VoicePunjabAI respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This privacy policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the VoicePunjabAI website.

Information We Collect

When you use VoicePunjabAI, the system may process text that you enter for the purpose of translation or text-to-speech generation.

We may temporarily store information such as:

• Text submitted for translation or speech
• Usage statistics (character counts)
• Anonymous request logs

This information is used only to improve performance and manage system usage.

How We Use Information

Information processed through VoicePunjabAI is used to:

• Generate Punjabi speech audio
• Translate English text to Punjabi
• Monitor usage limits
• Improve system performance

Data Storage

Some translated text or generated audio may be temporarily cached to improve performance and reduce processing time. Cached data is used only for technical optimization.

Third-Party Services

VoicePunjabAI uses trusted third-party services such as Google Cloud Text-to-Speech and Google Cloud Translation APIs to process audio and translation requests.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Contact page.
