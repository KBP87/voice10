How to Generate Correct Audio

1. If you already have Punjabi text

Paste your Punjabi Gurmukhi text directly into Option A and generate speech.

2. If you only have English text

Use Option B to convert English into Punjabi first. Then paste the Punjabi result into Option A.

3. If translation needs correction

Use the Punjabi keyboard to adjust spelling before generating speech.

4. Generate audio

Choose a voice and click Generate & Play to create your MP3 file.
